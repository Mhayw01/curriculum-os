Remembers why

Not just what you taught, but why you taught it that way.

Curriculum OS records the decisions you make in real time and the reasons behind them. You build up something teachers never usually have: a record of your reasoning over time, ready for when you need to show why you did what you did, and to see what actually worked.

Tue · Year 9
Slowed down on dividing fractions
Why: the do-now showed most pupils inverting the wrong fraction, so I remodelled it with bar models before moving on.
Wed · Year 7
Swapped silent practice for paired whiteboards
Why: too many misconceptions slipped through last lesson, so I wanted to see every pupil's working in real time.
Thu · Year 11
Dropped the extension for past-paper practice
Why: the mock flagged lost method marks, so timed exam questions mattered more than stretch this week.
